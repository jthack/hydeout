I've always been a fan of [levelsio](https://x.com/levelsio). He's a serial entrepreneur who has launched a bunch of SaaS and AI products, mostly in public, one after another. I think that's cool and I've always wanted to do the same. But I kept building things to 80% and never shipping them.

A few days ago I [tweeted this](https://x.com/rez0__/status/2070888396226969686?s=20) and decided I'm actually sticking to it. **The first launch is today**, and there will most definitely be more in the next few days.

## AI Safety For Parents

As a father of 3 kids who works in AI and security, I'm always getting tons of questions about AI from other parents. So I put all the answers in one place: **[aisafetyforparents.com](https://aisafetyforparents.com)**.

It's a simple 10-day, email-based course that "bottles up" everything you need to know about AI safety as a parent. I think you'll love it 😊

Social media had massive negative effects on kids that parents didn't see coming. My goal is to prevent that from happening with AI.

It would mean a lot to me if you shared it with people who might be interested.

This is the most "in my niche" of the 3 products I've built. The other two might surprise you!
